In exaltation Arjuna  is  exuberating  on  the  manifest  potencies  of  the
Supreme Lord Krishna exclaiming He is the wind god, the fire god, the  water
god, etc. and Brahma as well  as  being  the  grandsire  of  all.  Obeisance
thousands of times Arjuna is offering Him again and again.
