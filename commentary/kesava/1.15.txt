<p><div align=justify><p>
In  order  to  subtly  convey  the  superiority  of  the  Pandavas,  Sanjaya
expresses in this verse beginning with the word  <i>pancajanyam</i>  which  is  the
name of Lord Krishna's transcendental conch  shell  and  then  mentions  the
names of the divine conch shells of Arjuna and Bhima.
