Nevertheless in order  to  shower  His  grace  exclusively  upon  those  who
worship Him in <i>bhakti</i>  or  exclusive  loving  devotion,  He  <i>atma-bhava-stho</i>
meaning manifests within their hearts <i>jnana-dipena</i> the radiant knowledge  of
enlightenment which completely dispels all <i>tamah</i> or  darkness  of  ignorance
due to unlimited <i>karma</i> or multitudinous reactions from  actions  since  time
immemorial.
