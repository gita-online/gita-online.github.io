Beheld within Lord Krishna's <i>visvarupa</i> or divine  universal  form  was  seen
Brahma seated upon the lotus  emanating  from  the  navel  of  Garbhodaksayi
Vishnu, and also Shiva was seen with all the demigods like Indra, Surya  and
Vaya along with innumerable beings both  animate  and  inanimate  comprising
all species of sentient beings born from wombs, from eggs,  from  seeds  and
from fermentation.
