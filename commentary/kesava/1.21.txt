Then what happenned? To this question Sanjaya answers Dhritarastra in  these
two verses first by addressing him  as <i>O  descendant  of  Bharata.</i>  What  is
being subtly suggested here is that being born in  the  exalted  lineage  of
the righteous and noble King Bharata, it would not be  appropriate  for  the
blind Dhritarastra to betray his nephews the Pandavas who are the five  sons
of his brother.
