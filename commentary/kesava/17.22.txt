The auspiciousness of resonating TAT also  as  a  name  of  the  <i>brahman</i>  or
spiritual substratum pervading all existence which  is  a  potency  of  Lord
Krishna signifying that everything is under  the  complete  control  of  the
Supreme Lord. To perform prescribed Vedic activities without any desire  for
rewards is known as TAT. Such followers of the Vedic scriptures perform  all
activities for the Supreme Lord out of devotion or as a matter of duty  with
no expectation of anything in return. This is also known as TAT.
