In this way  Sanjaya  narrated  the  complete  divine  discourse  of  Srimad
Bhagavad-Gita and linking it to himself remarks how amazing it was  and  how
astounded he was by directly witnessing it. The  adjective  <i>mahatma</i>  meaning
great souls refers to Lord Krishna as well as all His devotees.
