The adjective <i>viryavan</i> meaning very powerful applies to Dhristiketu the  son
of Sisupala, Cekitana and the famous King of Kasi. <i>Purujit</i>   meaning  winner
of many battles refers  to  Kuntibhoga  while  <i>narapungava</i>  meaning  noblest
among men qualifies King Saibya.
