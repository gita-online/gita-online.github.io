<p><div align=justify><p>
That <i>sukham</i> or happiness which stupefies  the  <i>jiva</i>  or  embodied  being  by
making perception of reality obscure. Which seems to be like nectar  in  the
beginning as well as in the end and  which  is  supported  by  slothfulness,
indolence, folly and delusion is declared by Lord  Krishna  as  situated  in
<i>tama guna</i> the mode of ignorance.
