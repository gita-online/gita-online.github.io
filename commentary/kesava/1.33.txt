<p><div align=justify>Deliberating further Arjuna determines  that  when  unrighteousness  becomes
predominant in the family due to the loss of  the  father  who  insures  the
continuation of  the  family  customs  and  the  propagation  of  the  Vedic
tradition, the females of  the  family  become  easily  accessible  and  are
placed  in  conditions  of  compromise.  From  this  polluted  and  degraded
position arises undesirable progeny. The purpose of Arjuna  addressing  Lord
Krishna by the vocative <i>Varsneya</i> is to remind Him that He took birth in  the
exalted royal Vrsni dynasty and as such should be fully aware of  all  these
things.
