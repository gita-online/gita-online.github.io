<p><div align=justify>Other erudite men of knowledge assert that since all activities  contain  an
element of desire which are producive of bondage they should  all  be  given
up. While other wise  men  of  discernment  declare  that  prescribed  Vedic
duties such as <i>yagna</i> or  ritualistic  worship  and  propitiation,  <i>tapah</i>  or
austerities and penance and <i>danam</i> or charity should  never to be abandoned.
