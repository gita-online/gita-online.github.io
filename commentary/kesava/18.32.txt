Of the three types of <i>dhriti</i> or determination influenced by the three  <i>gunas</i>
or modes of material existence, Lord Krishna first begins with  <i>sattva  guna</i>
the mode of goodness. The proposed  activity  which  is  unwavering  in  its
resolve, focused in mind one pointedly without  deviation  or  cessation  by
utilising the <i>yoga</i> of meditation and <i>pranayama</i> or breath control  to  direct
the function of the senses such <i>dhriti</i>  is  invariably  situated  in  <i>sattva</i>
<i>guna.</i>
