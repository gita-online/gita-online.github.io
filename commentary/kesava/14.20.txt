Having transcended material existence in one's physical body  in  this  very
life through <i>atma tattva</i> or realisation ot  the  immortal  soul  within  the
etheric heart. One effortlessly rises above the  three  <i>gunas</i>  or  modes  of
material nature no longer influenced by them.  Lord  Krishna  confirms  that
when this transpires one is eternally freed from  birth,  old  age,  disease
and death as well as all the effects of material  nature  and  qualifies  to
enter the immortal spiritual realms forever in association with Him.
