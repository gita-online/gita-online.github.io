Among  annihilators  Lord  Krishna's  <i>vibhuti</i>  or   divine,   transcendental
opulence is <i>myrtuh</i> or all devouring death. Of the seven  transformations  of
the physical body His <i>vibhuti</i> is birth. Of the seven feminine  qualities  of
utmost virtue His <i>vibhuti</i> comprises them all as described.
