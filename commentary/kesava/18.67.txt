Even if one does not recite it themselves but  listens  to  others  reciting
Srimad Bhagavad-Gita. Such a  person  who  appreciates  the  words  of  Lord
Krishna and has faith in them and is not critical of a faulty  pronunciation
or  imperfect  meter.  Such  a  person  from  merely  hearing  this  divine
discourse will enter the joyous heavenly spheres at the cessation of life.
