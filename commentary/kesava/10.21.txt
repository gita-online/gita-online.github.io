Among the 11 Rudras or demigods of destruction, Lord  Krishna's  <i>vibhuti</i>  or
divine, transcendental opulence is <i>Sankara</i> which is synonymous  with  Shiva.
Of the Yakshas and Rakshasas, His <i>vibhuti</i> is <i>Kuvera</i> the lord of  wealth  and
treasurer of the demigods and of the 8 Vasus,  His  <i>vibhuti</i>  is  <i>Pavaka</i>  the
firegod Agni.
