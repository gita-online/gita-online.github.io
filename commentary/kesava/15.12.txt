The all pervading potency of Lord Krishna  is  in  the  Earth  by  which  He
permeates sustaining and nourishing  all  movable  and  immovable  <i>jivas</i>  or
embodied beings by His <i>ojasa</i> or irresistable  potency  and  bmanifesting  as
the ambrosial nectar of the moon he nourishes all plant life such as  herbs,
vegetables, fruits and grains.
