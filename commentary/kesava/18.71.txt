Sanjaya explains that he received the mercy of clairaudience  and  celestial
vision from Vedavyasa,  the  author  of  Vedanta  Sutras,  Mahabharata,  the
Puranas, etc.  Sanjaya  considers  himself  most  blessed  by  hearing  Lord
Krishna speak the divine discourse of Srimad Bhagavad-Gita.
