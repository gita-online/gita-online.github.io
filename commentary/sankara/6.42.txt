<p><i>Athava</i>, or; <i>bhavati</i>, he is born; <i>kule</i>, in the
family; <i>dhimatam</i>, of wise; <i>yoginam</i>, yogis; <i>eva</i>,
only, who are poor-which is different from the
family of the prosperous. <i>Etat janma</i>, such a birth;
<i>yat idrsam</i>, as is of this kind-a birth that is in the
family of poor yogis, in a family as described; is <i>hi</i>,
surely; <i>durlabha-taram</i>, more difficult to get, as
compared with the earlier one; <i>loke</i>, in the world.</p>
