<i>Vrsninam</i>, of the Vrsnis, Yadavas, the tribe which Krishna was born in. I am Vasudeva - I who am this 
person, your friend. <i>Pandavanam</i>, of the Pandavas, (I am) <i>Dhananjaya</i>, you yourself. <i>Api</i>, and; 
<i>muninam</i>, of the wise, of the thoughtful, of those who know of all things, I am Vyasa. <i>kavinam</i>, of 
the omniscient (i.e. of the those who know the past, present and future), I am the omniscient <i>Usanas</i> 
(Sukracarya).
