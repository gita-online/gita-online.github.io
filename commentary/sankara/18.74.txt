<i>Aham</i>, I; <i>iti</i>, thus; <i>asrausam</i>, heard; <i>imam</i>, this; <i>samvadam</i>, 
conversation, as has been narrated; <i>vasudevasya</i>, of Vasudeva; and <i>mahatmanah, parthasya</i>, 
of the great-soulded Partha; which is <i>adbhutam</i>, unique, extremely wonderful; and 
<i>roma-harsanam</i>, makes one's hair stand on end.
