Foods that are <i>katu-amla-lavana-atyusna tiksna-ruksa-vidahinah</i>, bitter, sour, salty, very hot, pungent, dry (without fat) and burning; and 
<i>duhkha-soka amaya-pradah</i>, which produce pain, sorrow and disease; (Pain, immediate suffering; sorrow, griefarising from not having that desired food) 
are <i>rajasasyaistah</i>, dear to one having rajas.
