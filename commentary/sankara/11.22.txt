<i>Ye</i>, those who are; the <i>rudra-adityah</i>, Rudras and Adityas; <i>vasavah</i>, the Vasus; and 
<i>sadhyah</i>, the Sadhyas - the groups of Rudras and other gods; the gods visve, Visve-devas; and <i>asvinau</i>, 
the two Asvins; <i>marutah</i>, the Maruts; and <i>usmapah</i>, the Usmapas, (a class of) manes; and 
<i>gandharva yaksa-asura-siddha-sanghah</i>, hosts of Gandharvas - viz Haha, Huhu and others, Yaksas - viz 
Kubera and others, demons - Virocana and others, and Siddhas-Kapila and others; <i>sarve eva</i>, all of those 
very ones; <i>viksante</i>, gaze; tva, (i.e.) <i>tvam</i>, at You; <i>vismitah eva</i>, being indeed struck with 
wonder.
