<i>Sattvat</i>, from sattva, when it predominates; <i>sanjayate</i>, is born; <i>jnanam</i>, knowledge; and <i>rajasah</i>, from rajas; is 
verily born <i>lobhah</i>, avarice. <i>Tamasah</i>, from tamas; <i>bhavatah</i>, are born; both <i>pramada mohau</i>, in-advertence and delusion; 
as also <i>ajnanam</i>, ignorance; <i>eva ca</i>, to be sure.
