<i>Yah antah-sukhah</i>, one who is happy within, in the indwelling Self; and so also <i>antar-aramah</i>, has pleasure within - he 
disports only in the Self within; similarly, <i>antar-jyotih eva</i>, has his light only within, has the indwelling Self alone as 
his light; <i>sah yogi</i>, that yogi; yah, who is of this kind; <i>brahma-bhutah</i>, having become Brahman, even while he is 
still living; <i>adhigacchati</i>, attains; <i>brahma-nirvanam</i>, absorption in Brahman, gets Liberation.
