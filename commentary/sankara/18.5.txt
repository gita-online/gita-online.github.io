<p>
    <i>Yajna-dana-tapah-karma</i>, the practice of sacrifice, charity and austerity-this threefold practice; 
    <i>na tyajyam</i>, is not to be abandoned; <i>tat</i>, it; is <i>eva</i>, surely; <i>karyam</i>, 
    to be undertaken. 
</p>

<p>
    Why? <i>Yajnah</i>, sacrifice; <i>danam</i>, charity; and <i>tapah</i>, austerity; are <i>eva</i>, 
    verily; <i>pavanani</i>, the purifiers, the causes of sanctification; manisinam, of the wise, i.e. .
    of those who do not seek results for themselves.
</p>
