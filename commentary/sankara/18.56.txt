<i>Sada</i>, ever; <i>kurvanah api</i>, engaging even in; <i>sarva-karmani</i>, all 
actions, even the prohibited ones; <i>madvyapasrayah</i>, one to whom I am the refuge, 
to whom I, Vasudeva the Lord, am the refuge, i.e. one who has totally surrendered himself 
to Me; even he, <i>apnoti</i>, attains; the <i>sasvatam</i>, eternal; <i>avyayam</i>, 
immutable; padam, State of Visnu; <i>mat-prasadat</i>, through My, i.e. God's, grace.
