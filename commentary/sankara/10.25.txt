<i>Maharsinam</i>, among the great sages, I am <i>Bhrgu</i>; <i>Giram</i>, of words, 
of utterances, in the form of words; I am the <i>ekam</i>, single; <i>aksaram</i>, 
syllable Om. <i>Yajnanam</i>, among rituals; I am the <i>japa yajnah</i>, rituals of 
Japa. <i>Sthavaranam</i>, of the immovables, I am the Himalaya.
