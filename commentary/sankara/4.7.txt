O scion of the Bharata dynasty, <i>yada yada hi</i>, 
whenever; <i>bhavati</i>, there is; a <i>glanih</i>, decline, 
decrease; <i>dharmasya</i>, of virtue consisting of the 
duties of men and stages of life of living beings, 
which are the means to achieving properity and 
Liberation; and <i>abhyutthanam</i>, increase, rise; 
<i>adharmasya</i>, of unrighteousness; <i>tada</i>, 
then; do <i>aham</i>, I; <i>srjami</i>, manifest; <i>atmanam</i>, 
Myself, through Maya.<br><br>
