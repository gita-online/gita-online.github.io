<i>Tasmat</i>, therefore; <i>yajna-dana-tapah, kriyah</i>, acts of sacrifice, charity and austerity-acts in the form of sacrifice etc.; <i>vidhana-uktah</i>, as 
prescribed through injunctions, as ordained by the scriptures; <i>brahma-vadinam</i>, of those who study and expound the Vedas; <i>satatam</i>, always; 
<i>pravartante</i>, commence; <i>udahrtya</i>, after uttering; <i>om iti</i>, the syllable Om.
