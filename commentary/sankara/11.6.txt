<i>Pasya</i>, see; <i>adityan</i>, the twelve Adityas; <i>vasun</i>, the eight Vasus; <i>rudran</i>, the 
eleven Rudras; <i>asvinau</i>, the two Asvins; and <i>amarutah</i>, the Maruts, who are divided into 
seven groups of seven each. <i>Bharata</i>, O scion of the Bharata dynasty; <i>pasya</i>, behold; <i>tatha</i>, 
also; <i>bahuni</i>, the many other; <i>ascaryani</i>, wonders; <i>adrstapurvani</i>, not seen before - by you 
or anyone else in thehuman world.
