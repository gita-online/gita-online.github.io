<i>Yat yat</i>, whatever; <i>sattvam</i>, object in the world; is <i>eva</i>, verily; <i>vibhutimat</i>, endowed 
with majesty; <i>srimad</i>, possessed of prosperity; <i>va</i>, or; is <i>urjitam</i>, energetic, possessed of 
vigour; <i>tvam</i>, you; <i>avagaccha</i>, know; <i>eva</i>, for certain; <i>tat tat</i>, each of them; as 
<i>mama tejomsa-sambhavam</i>, having a part (amsa) of My (mama), of God's, power (teja) as its source (sambhavam).
