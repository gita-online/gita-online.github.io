<p>
    <i>Gitam</i>, It has been sung of, spoken of; <i>bahudha</i>, in various ways; <i>rsibhih</i>, by the Rsis, 
    by Vasistha and others; sung <i>prthak</i>, separately; <i>vividhaih</i>, by the different kinds of; 
    <i>chandobhih</i>, Vedic texts-chandas mean the Rg-veda etc; by them; <i>ca</i>, and; besides, 
    <i>hetumadbhih</i>, by the rational; and <i>viniscitaih</i>, by the convincing, i.e. by those which are 
    productive of certain knowledge not by those which are in an ambiguous form; <i>brahma-sutra-padaih eva</i>, 
    sentences themselves which are indicative of and lead to Brahman. Brahma-sutras are the sentences 
    indicative of Brahman. 
</p>

<p>
    They are called padani since Brahman is reached, known, through them. By them 
    indeed has been sung the true nature of the field and the Knower of the field (this is understood). The 
    Self is verily known through such sentences as, 'The Self alone is to be meditated upon' (Br. 1.4.7), 
    which are indicative of and lead to Brahman.
</p>
