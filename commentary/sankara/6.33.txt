<p>O <i>Madhusudana</i>, <i>ayam</i>, this; <i>yogah</i>, Yoga; <i>yah
proktah</i>, that has been spoken of; <i>tvaya</i>, by You;
<i>samyena</i>, as sameness; <i>na pasyami</i>, I do not see, I
cannot conceive; -what?-<i>etasya</i>, its; <i>sthiram</i>, steady,
undisturbed; <i>sthitim</i>, continuance; <i>cancalatvat</i>,
owing to the unsteadiness of the mind, which is
well known.</p>
