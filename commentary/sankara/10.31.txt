<i>Pavatam</i>, of the purifiers; I am <i>pavanah</i>, air. <i>Sastra-bhrtam</i>, 
among wielders of weapons, I am Rama, son of Dasaratha. Jhasanam, among fishes 
etc; I am the particular species of fish called makarah shark. I am <i>jahnavi</i>, 
Ganga; <i>srotasam</i>, among rivers, among streams of water.
