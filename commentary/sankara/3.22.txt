O Partha, <i>na asti</i>, there is no; <i>kartavyam</i>, duty; <i>kincana</i>, whatsoever; me, for Me (to fulfill); even 
<i>trisu lokesu</i>, in all the three worlds.<br><br>

Why?<br><br>

There is <i>na anavaptam</i>, nothing (that remains) unachieved; or <i>avaptavyam</i>, to be achieved. Still <i>varte eva</i>, 
do I continue; <i>karmani</i>, in action.
