<i>Hi</i>, for; O son of Prtha, <i>ye api</i>, even those; <i>papayonayah syuh</i>, who 
are born of sin; as to who they are, the Lord says - <i>striyah</i>, women; vaisyah, 
Money-Lenders, Merchants, <i>tatha</i>, as also; sudrah, lower class people; 
<i>te api</i>, even they; <i>yanti</i>, reach, go to; the <i>param</i>, highest; 
<i>gatim</i>, Goal <i>vyapasritya</i>, by taking shelter; <i>mam</i>, under Me - by 
accepting Me as their refuge.
