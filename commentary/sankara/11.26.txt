<i>Ca</i>, and; <i>tvam</i>, into You - this is to be connected with 'rapidly enter' in the next verse; <i>sarve</i>, 
all; <i>ami</i>, those; <i>putrah</i>, sons - Duryodhana and others; <i>dhrtarastrasya</i>, of Dhrtarastra; 
<i>saha</i>, along with; <i>avanipala-sanghaih</i>, multitudes of the rulers (pala) of the earth (avani); also 
Bhisma, Drona, <i>tatha</i>, and; <i>asau</i>, that; <i>suta-putrah</i>, son of a Suta, Karna; <i>saha</i>, 
together with; <i>api</i>, even; <i>asmadiyaih</i>, our; <i>yodha-mukhyaih</i>, prominent warriors, the 
commanders - Dhrstadyumna and others.
