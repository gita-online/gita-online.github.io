<p><i>The yogi</i>, the man of Knowledge; <i>yatamanah</i>,
applying himself; <i>prayatnat</i>, assiduously, i.e.
striving more intensely; and as a result,
<i>samsuddha-kilbisah</i>, becoming purified from sin;
and <i>aneka-janma-samsiddhah,</i> attaining perfection
through many births - gathering together
tendencies little by little in many births, and
attaining perfection through that totality of
impressions acquired in many births; <i>tatah,</i>
thereby coming to have full Illumination; <i>yati,</i>
achieves; <i>the param</i>, highest, most perfect; <i>gatim</i>,
Goal.</p>
