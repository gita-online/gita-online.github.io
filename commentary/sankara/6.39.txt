<p><i>O Krsna</i>, You should; <i>arhasi</i>, certainly; <i>asesatah</i>, completely;
<i>chettum</i>, remove, eliminate; <i>etat</i>, this; <i>samsayam</i>,
doubt; <i>me</i>, of mine. <i>Hi</i>, indeed; <i>na tvad anyah</i>, there is no other,
be it a sage or a god; <i>upapadyate</i>, who can be; <i>chetta</i>, the dispeller, the remover; <i>asya</i>, of
this; <i>samsayasya</i>, doubt. Therefore, You Yourself
should dispel (the doubt).</p>
