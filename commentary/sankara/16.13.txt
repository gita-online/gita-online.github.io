<i>Idam</i>, this thing; <i>labham</i>, has been gained; <i>maya</i>, by me; <i>adya</i>, today; <i>prapsye</i>, I shall acquire; <i>idam</i>, this other; 
<i>manoratham</i>, desired object which is delectable to the mind. And <i>idam</i>, this; <i>asti</i>, is in hand; <i>punah</i>, again; <i>idam</i>, this; 
<i>dhanam</i>, wealth; <i>api</i>, also; <i>bhavisyati</i>, will come; me, to me, in the next year. Thereby I shall become rich and famous.
