That joy is <i>udahrtam</i>, said to be; <i>tamasam</i>, born of tamas; <i>yat</i>, which; both <i>agre</i>, in 
the beginning; ca, and; anubandhe, in the sequel, after the end (of enjoyment); is <i>mohanam</i>, delusive; 
<i>atmanah</i>, to oneself; and <i>nidra-alasya-pramada uttham</i>, arises from sleep, laziness and inadvertence. 
Therefore, now is begun a verse in order to conclude this section.
