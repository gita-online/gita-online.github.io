If <i>asamarthah asi</i>, you are unable; <i>api</i>, even; <i>abhyase</i>, to practise; then, <i>bhava</i>, be; <i>mat-karma paramah</i>, intent 
on works for Me - works (karma) meant for Me (mat) are mat-karma i.e., you be such that works meant for Me become most important to you. In the 
absence of Practice, <i>api</i>, even; <i>kurvan</i>, by undertaking; <i>karmani</i>, works alone; <i>madartham</i>, for Me; <i>avapsyasi</i>, 
you will attain; <i>siddhim</i>, perfection - by gradually acquiring purification of mind, concentration and Knowledge.
