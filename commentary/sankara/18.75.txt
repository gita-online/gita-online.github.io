And <i>vyasa-prasadat</i>, through the favour of Vyasa, by having received divine vision from him; 
<i>aham</i>, I; <i>srutvan</i>, heard; etat, <i>this</i>; <i>guhyam</i>, secret dialogue, such as it 
is; concerning the <i>param</i>, supreme; <i>Yogam</i>, Yoga-or, this dialogue itself is the Yoga 
because it is meant for it; <i>krsnat</i>, from Krsna; <i>yogeswarat</i>, from the Lord of yogas; 
<i>kathayatah</i>, while He was speaking; <i>svayam</i>, Himself; <i>saksat</i>, actually; not 
indirectly through others.
