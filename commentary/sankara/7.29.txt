<p><i>Ye</i>, those who; <i>yatanti</i>, strive; <i>asritya</i>, by
resorting; <i>mam</i>, to Me, the supreme God, by
having their minds absorbed in Me; <i>jara-maranamoksaya</i>,
for becoming free from old age and
death; <i>te</i>, they; <i>viduh</i>, know; <i>tat</i>, that; <i>brahma</i>,
Brahman, which is the Supreme; they know
<i>krtsnam</i>, everything; about <i>adhyatmam</i>, the
individual Self, that indwelling entity; <i>ca</i>, and; they
know <i>akhiliam</i>, all; about <i>karma</i>, actions.</p>
