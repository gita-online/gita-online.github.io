<i>Asmi</i>, I am; the <i>loka-ksaya-krt</i>, world destroying; <i>kalah</i>, Time; <i>pravrddhah</i>, grown in 
stature. Hear the purpose for which I have grown in stature: I am <i>iha</i>, now; <i>pravrttah</i>, engaged; 
<i>samahartum</i>, in annihilating; <i>lokan</i>, the creatures. <i>Api</i>, even; <i>rte tva</i>, without you; 
<i>sarve</i>, all - from whom your apprehension had arisen; the <i>yodhah</i>, warriors - Bhisma, Drona, Karna 
and others; <i>ye</i>, who are; <i>avasthitah</i>, arrayed; <i>pratyanikesu</i>, in the confronting armies - in 
every unit of the army confronting the other; <i>na bhavisyanti</i>, will cease to exist.
