<p>
    <i>Asya</i>, of this; <i>jagatah</i>, world; <i>aham</i>, I; am <i>pita</i>, the father; 
    <i>mata</i>, the mother; <i>dhata</i>, ordainer, dispenser of the results of their 
    actions to the creatures; (and the) <i>pirtamahah</i>, grand-father. 
</p>

<p>
    I am the vedayam, knowable - that which has to be known; the <i>pavitram</i>, 
    sanctifier; and the onkarah, syllable Om; <i>eva ca</i>, as also Rk, Sama and Yajus. 
</p>
