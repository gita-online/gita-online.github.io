<i>Vedanam</i>, among the Vedas; I am the Sama veda. <i>Devanam</i>, among the gods - 
such as Rudras, Adityas and others; I am <i>vasavah</i>, Indra. <i>Indriyanam</i>, 
among the eleven organs, viz eye etc.; I am the <i>manah</i>, mind. I am the mind 
which is of the nature of reflection and doubt. And I am the <i>cetana</i>, intelligence,
It is the medium for the manifestation of Consciousness, the function of the intellect 
ever manifest in the aggregate of body and organs; <i>bhtanam</i>, in creatures.
