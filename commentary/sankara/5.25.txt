<i>Rsayah</i>, the seers, those who have full realization, the monks; <i>ksina-kalmasah</i>, whose sins, defects like sin etc., 
have been attenuated; <i>chinna-dvaidhah</i>, who are freed from doubt; <i>yata atmanah</i>, whose organs are under control; 
<i>ratah</i>, who are engaged; <i>sarvabhutahite</i>, in doing good to all beings - favourably disposed towards all, i.e. 
harmless; <i>labhante</i>, attain; <i>brahma-nirvanam</i>, absorption in Brahman, Liberation.
