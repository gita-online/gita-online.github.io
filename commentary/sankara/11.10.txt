A form <i>aneka-vaktra-nayanam</i>, having many faces and eyes; <i>aneka-adbhuta-darsanam</i>, possessing many 
wonderful sights; as also <i>aneka divya-abharanam</i>, adorned with numerous celestial ornaments; and 
<i>divya-aneka-udyata ayudham</i>, holding many uplifted heavenly weapons. This whole portion is connected 
with the verb '(He) showed' in the earlier verse.
