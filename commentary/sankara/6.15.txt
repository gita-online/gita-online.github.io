<p><i>Yunjan</i>, concentrating; <i>atmanam</i>, the mind;
<i>evam</i>, thus, according to the methods shown
above; <i>sada</i>, forever; the yogi, <i>niyata-manasah</i>, of
controlled mind; <i>adhi-gacchati</i>, achieves; <i>santim</i>,
the Peace, the indifference to worldly attachments
and possessions; <i>nirvana-paramam</i>, which
culminates in Liberation; and <i>mat-samstham</i>,
which abides in Me. Now are being mentioned the
rules about the yogi's food, etc., in the next verse.</p>
