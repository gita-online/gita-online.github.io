<i>Tu</i>, but; the <i>danam</i>, gift; <i>yat</i>, which; <i>diyate</i>, is made; <i>prati-upakara-artham</i>, expecting reciprocation - with this purpose 
in view: 'In time, he will render service in return'; <i>va punah</i>, or again; <i>uddisya</i>, with a desire for; its <i>phalam</i>, result that, 'To 
me will accrue some unseen reward of this gift'; and which is <i>diyate</i>, given; <i>pariklistam</i>, grudgingly, with reluctance; w, that; is 
<i>smrtam</i>, considered to be; rajasam, born of rajas.
