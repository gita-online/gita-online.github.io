And, <i>rajan</i>, O King; <i>samsmrtya samsmrtya</i>, repeatedly recollecting; <i>tat</i>, that; 
<i>ati-adbhutam</i>, greatly extraordinary; <i>rupam</i>, form, the Cosmic form; <i>hareh</i>, of 
the Lord; <i>mahan vismayah me</i>, I am struck with great wonder. And <i>hrsyami</i>, I rejoice; 
<i>punah punah</i>, again and again.
