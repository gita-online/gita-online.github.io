O Visnu, <i>hi</i>, verily; <i>drstva</i>, seeing; <i>tvam</i>, You; <i>nabhah-sprsam</i>, touching heaven; 
<i>diptam</i>, blazing; <i>aneka-varnam</i>, with many colours, (i.e.) possessed of many frightening forms; 
<i>vyatta-ananam</i>, open mouthed; <i>dipta-visala-netram</i>, with firey large eyes; I, <i>pravyathita-antara-atma</i>, 
becoming terrified in my mind; <i>na vindami</i>, do not find; <i>dhrtim</i>, steadiness; <i>ca</i>, and; 
<i>samam</i>, peace, calmness of mind. Why?
