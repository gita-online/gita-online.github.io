<i>Ye</i>, those; <i>janah</i>, persons; who <i>tapyante</i>, undertake, perform; <i>ghoram</i>, severe; <i>tapah</i>, austerity, productive of pain to oneself as 
also to creatures; <i>asastra-vihitam</i>, not sanctioned by the scriptures; they, being <i>dambh-ahankara-samyuktah</i>, given to ostentation and pride; and 
<i>kama-raga-bala-anvitah</i>, impelled by the strength of passion and attachment, or possessed of passion, attachment and strength.
