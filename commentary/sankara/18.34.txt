<i>Tu</i>, but, O Partha; the <i>dhrtya</i>, firmness; <i>yaya</i>, with which; a person <i>dharayate</i>, 
holds on to; <i>dharma-kama-arthan</i>, righteousness, covetable things and wealth-entertains the conviction 
in the mind that these ought to be pursued always; and becomes <i>phala-akanksi</i>, desirous of their fruits; 
<i>prasangena</i>, as the occasion for each arises, according as the situation arises for holding on to any 
one of dharma etc.; <i>sa</i>, that; <i>dhrtih</i>, firmness; is <i>rajasi</i>, born of rajas.
