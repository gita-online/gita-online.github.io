<i>Paritranaya</i>, for the protection; <i>sadhunam</i>, of 
the pious, the followers of the virtuous path; 
<i>vinasaya</i>, for the destruction; <i>duskrtam</i>, of the evil-doers, 
of the sinful ones; and also <i>dharmasamsthapanarthaya</i>, for 
establishing virtue fully; for that purpose, <i>sambhavami</i>, 
I manifest Myself; <i>yuge yuge</i>, in every age.
