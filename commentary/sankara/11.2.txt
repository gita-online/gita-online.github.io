<i>Kamala-partraksa</i>, O You with eyes like lotus leaves; <i>bhava-apyayau</i>, the origin and dissolution 
these two; <i>bhutanam</i>, of beings; <i>srutau</i>, have been heard; <i>maya</i>, by me; <i>vistarasah</i>, 
in detail - not in brief; <i>tvattah</i>, from You. <i>Ca</i>, and; (Your) <i>avyayam</i>, undecaying; 
<i>mahatmyam</i>, glory, too; has been heard (these last words) remain understood.
