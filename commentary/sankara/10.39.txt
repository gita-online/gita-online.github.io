<i>Ca</i>, moreover; O Arjuna, <i>yat api</i>, whatsoever; is the <i>bijam</i>, seed, the source of growth; 
<i>sarva bhutanam</i>, of all beings; <i>tat</i>, that I am. As a conclusion of the topic the Lord states in 
brief His divine manifestations: <i>Na tat asti bhutam</i>, there is no thing; <i>cara-acaram</i>, moving or 
non-moving; yat, which; syat, can exist; vina maya, without Me. For whatever is rejected by Me, from whatever 
I withdraw Myself will have no substance, and will become a non-entity. Hence the meaning is that everything 
has Me as its essence.
