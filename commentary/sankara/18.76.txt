And, <i>rajan</i>, O king, Dhrtarastra; after having heard, <i>samsmrtya samsmrtya</i>, while 
repeatedly remembering; <i>imam</i>, this; <i>adbhuttam</i>, unique; <i>samvadam</i>, dialogue; 
<i>kesava-arjunayoh</i>, between Kesava and Arjuna; which is <i>punyam</i>, sacred, removes sin 
even when heard; <i>hrsyami</i>, I rejoice; <i>muhuh, muhuh</i>, every moment.
