<p>
    O Visnu, Your fierce rays are acorching; <i>Lelihyase</i>, You lick Your lips, You taste; <i>grasamanah</i>, 
    while devouring, while taking in; <i>samagran</i>, all; <i>lokan</i>, the creatures; <i>samantat</i>, from 
    all sides; <i>jvaladbhih</i>, with flaming; <i>vadanaih</i>, mouths; which are <i>apurya</i>, completely 
    filling; <i>samagram</i>, the whole - together (saha) with the foremost (agrena); <i>jagat</i>, world; 
    <i>tejobhih</i>, with heat. 
</p>

<p>
    Moreover, O Visnu, the all-pervading One, <i>tava</i>, Your; <i>ugrah</i>, fierce; <i>bhasah</i>, rays; 
    are <i>pratapanti</i>, scorching. Since You are of such a terrible nature.
</p>
