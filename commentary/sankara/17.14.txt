<i>Deva-dvija-guru-pujanam</i>, the worship of gods, twice-borns, venerable persons and the wise; <i>saucam</i>, purity; <i>arjavam</i>, straightforwardness; 
<i>brahmacarayam</i>, celibacy; and <i>ahimsa</i>, non-injury; <i>ucyate</i>, are said to be; <i>sariram</i>, bodily; <i>tapah</i>, austerity, austerity 
accomplished through the body: that which can be performed by the agent, etc., (i.e.) with the whole group of body and organs, in which the body 
predominates; for the Lord will say, 'these five are its causes' (18.15).
