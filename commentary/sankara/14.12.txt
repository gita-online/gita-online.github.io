O best of the Bharata dynasty, when the quality of rajas <i>vivrddhe</i>, becomes predominant; <i>etani</i>, these indications; 
<i>jayante</i>, come into being; <i>lobhah</i>, avarice, the desire to appropriate other's possessions; <i>pravrtih</i>, 
movement in general; <i>arambhah</i>, undertaking; Of what? <i>karmanam</i>, of actions; <i>asamah</i>, unrest, lack of tranquillity 
(i.e.) manifestation of joy, attachment, etc.; and <i>sprha</i>, hankering, desire in general for all things.
