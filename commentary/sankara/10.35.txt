I am <i>tatha</i>, also; the <i>Brhat-sama</i>, the foremost <i>samnam</i>, of the Sama-mantras. <i>Chandasam</i>, 
of the metres, of the Rk-mantras having the metres Gayatri etc.; I am the Rk called Gayatri. This is the meaning. 
<i>Masanam</i>, of the months, I am Marga sirsa (Agrahayana, November-December). <i>Rtunam</i>, of the seasons; 
<i>kusumakarah</i>, spring.
