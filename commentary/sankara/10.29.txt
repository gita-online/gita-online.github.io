<i>Naganam</i>, among snakes, of a particular species of snakes; <i>asmi</i>, I am 
<i>Ananta</i>, the King of snakes. And <i>Varuna</i>, the King yadasam, of the 
gods of the waters. <i>Pitrnam</i>, among the ancestors; I am the King of the 
ancestors, named <i>Aryama</i>. And <i>samyamatam</i>, among the maintainers of 
law and order I am Yama.
