<i>Aharah</i>, foods; <i>ayuh-sattva-bala-arogya sukha-priti-vivardhanah</i>, that augment life, firmneess of mind, strength, health delight; and which are rasyah, 
succulent; <i>snigdhah</i>, oleaginous; <i>sthirah</i>, substantial, lasing in the body for long; and <i>hrdyah</i>, agreeable, to one's liking; are <i>sattvika-priyah</i>, 
dear to one endowed with sattva.
