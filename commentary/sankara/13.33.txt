<i>Yatha</i>, as; <i>sarva-gatam</i>, the all-pervading; <i>akasam</i>, space;-though pervasive, still, <i>na upalipyate</i>, is not defiled, 
does not come into contact; <i>saukmyat</i>, because of its subtlety; <i>tatha</i>, similarly; <i>atma</i>, the Self; <i>avasthitah</i>, 
present, sarvatra, everywhere; <i>dehe</i>, in the body; <i>na</i>, is not; <i>upalipyate</i>, defiled.
