<i>Pasyami</i>, I see; <i>tvam</i>, You; <i>aneka-bahu-udara vaktra-netram</i>, as possessed of numerous arms, 
bellies, mouths and eyes; <i>ananta-rupam</i>, having infinite forms; <i>sarvatah</i>, all around. Visveswara, 
O Lord of the Universe; <i>visva-rupa</i>, O Cosmic Person; <i>na pasyami</i>, I see not; <i>tava</i>, Your; 
<i>antam</i>, end; <i>na madhyam</i>, nor the middle - what lies between two extremities; <i>na punah</i>, 
nor again; the <i>adim</i>, beginning - I see not the limit (end) nor the middle, nor again the beginning, of 
You who are God!
