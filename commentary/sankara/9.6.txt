<i>Upadharaya</i>, understand; <i>iti</i>, thus; that <i>yatha</i>, just as; 
in the world, the <i>mahan</i>, voluminous-in dimension; <i>vayuh</i>, wind; 
<i>sarvatragah</i>, moving everywhere; is <i>nityam</i>, ever; <i>akasa-sthitah</i>, 
present in space; <i>tatha</i>, similarly; (sarvani, all; bhutani, beings; matsthani,) 
abide in Me who am omnipresent like space-abide certainly without any contact.
