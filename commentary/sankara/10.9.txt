<i>Maccittah</i>, with minds fixed on Me; <i>mad-gata pranah</i>, with lives (pranas) 
dedicated to Me, or having their organs, eyes etc. absorbed in Me, i.e. having their 
organs withdrawn into Me; <i>bodhayantah</i>, enlightening; <i>parasparam</i>, each other; 
and <i>nityam</i>, always; <i>kathayantah</i>, speaking of; <i>mam</i>, Me, as possessed 
of qualities like knowledge, strength, valour, etc; <i>tusyanti</i>, they derive satisfaction; 
and <i>ramanti</i>, rejoice, get happiness, as by coming in contact with a dear one.
