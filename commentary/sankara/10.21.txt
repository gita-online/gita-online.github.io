<i>Adityanam</i>, among the twelve Adityas; <i>aham</i>, I; am the Aditya called Visnu. 
<i>Jyotisam</i>, among the luminaries; <i>amsuman</i>, the radiant; <i>ravih</i>, sun. 
<i>Marutam</i>, among the different gods called Maruts; <i>asmi</i>, I am; the one 
called Marici. <i>Naksatranam</i>, among the stars; I am <i>sasi</i>, the moon.
