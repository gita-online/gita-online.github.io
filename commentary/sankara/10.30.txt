<i>Daityanam</i>, among demons, the descendants of Diti, I am the one called 
<i>Prahlada</i>. And I am <i>kalah</i>, Time; <i>kalayatam</i>, among reckoners 
of time, of those who calculate. And <i>mrganam</i>, among animals; I am 
<i>mrgendrah</i>, the Lion, or the tiger. And <i>paksinam</i>, among birds; 
(I am) <i>vainateyah</i>, Garuda, the son of Vinata.
