Seated in the body, it <i>upasevate</i>, enjoys; <i>visayan</i>, the objects-sound etc.; <i>adhisthaya</i>, by presiding over; <i>srotram</i>, the ear; 
<i>caksuh</i>, eyes; <i>sparsanam</i>, skin, the organ of touch; <i>rasanam</i>, tongue; <i>eva ca</i>, as also; the <i>ghranam</i>, nose; and <i>manah</i>,
