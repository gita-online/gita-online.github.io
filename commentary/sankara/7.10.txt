<p>O Partha, <i>viddhi</i>, know, <i>mam</i>, Me; to be the
<i>sanatanam</i>, eternal; <i>bijam</i>, seed, the source of
growth; <i>sarva-bhutanam</i>, of all beings. Besides, I
am the <i>buddhih</i>, intellect, the power of
discrimination of the mind; <i>buddhimatam</i>, of the
intelligent, of people having the power of
discrimination. I am the <i>tejah</i>, courage; <i>tejasvinam</i>,
of the courageous, of those possessed of that.</p>
