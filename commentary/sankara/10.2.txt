<i>Na sura-sanah</i>, neither the gods-Brahma and others; <i>viduh</i>, know; What do they 
not know? <i>me</i>, My; <i>prabhavam</i> (prabhavam), majesty, abundance of lordly power - 
or, derived in the sense of 'coming into being', it means origin. Nor even the 
<i>maharsayah</i>, great sages, Bhrgu and others (Bhrgu, Marici, Atri, Pulastya, Pulaha, 
Kratu and Vasistha) <i>devanam</i>, of the gods; <i>ca</i>, and; <i>maharsinam</i>, of 
the great sages.
