<i>Yada</i>, when; <i>deha-bhrt</i>, an embodied one, the soul; <i>yati</i>, undergoes; <i>pralayam</i>, death; <i>sattve pravrddhe</i>, 
while sattva is predominant; <i>tu</i>, exclusively; <i>tada</i>, then; <i>pratipadyate</i>, he attains, i.e. gains; the <i>amalan</i>, 
tainless, stainless; <i>lokan</i>, worlds; <i>uttamavidam</i>, of those who know the highest, i.e. of those who have known the principles-
mahat and the rest.
