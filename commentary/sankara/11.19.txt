<i>Pasyami</i>, I see; <i>tvam</i>, You; as <i>anadi-madhya antam</i>, without beginning, middle and end; 
<i>ananta-viryam</i>, possessed of infinite valour; and also <i>ananta-bahum</i>, having innumerable arms; 
<i>sasi-surya-netram</i>, having the sun and the moon as the eyes; <i>dipta-hutasavakram</i>, having a mouth 
like a blazing fire; <i>tapantam</i>, heating up; <i>idam</i>, this; <i>visvam</i>, Universe; <i>sva-tejasa</i>, 
by Your own brilliance.
