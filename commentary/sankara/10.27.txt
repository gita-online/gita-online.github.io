<i>Asvanam</i>, among horses; <i>viddhi</i>, know; <i>mam</i>, Me; to be the 
horse named <i>Uccaihsravas</i>; <i>amrta-udbhavam</i>, born of nectar-born 
when (the sea was) churned (by the gods) for nectar. <i>Airavata</i>, the son 
of Iravati, <i>gajendranam</i>, among the Lordly elephants; 'know Me to be 
so' remains understood. And <i>naranam</i>, among men; know Me as the 
<i>naradhipam</i>, King of men.
