<i>Pralayam gatva</i>, when one does; <i>rajasi</i>, while the quality of rajas predominates; <i>jayate</i>, he is born; <i>karma-sangisu</i>, 
among people attached to activity, among human beings having attachment to work. <i>Tatha</i>, similarly, in that very way; <i>pralinah</i>, 
when one dies; <i>tamasi</i>, while tamas predominates; <i>jayate</i>, he takes birth; <i>mudha-yonisu</i>, among the stupid species, such 
as animals etc. A summary of the idea of the preceding (three) verses is being stated in the next verse.
