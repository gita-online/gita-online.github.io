<p><i>Uttamam</i>, supreme, unsurpassable; <i>sukham</i>,
Bliss; <i>upaiti</i>, comes; <i>hi enam yoginam</i>, to this yogi
alone; <i>prasanta-manasam</i>, whose mind has become
perfectly tranquil; <i>santa-rejasam</i>, whose (quality
of) rajas has been eliminated, i.e. whose rajas, viz
defects such as delusion etc. have been destroyed; <i>brahmabhutam</i>,
who has become identified with Brahman,
who is free even while living, who has got the
certitude that Brahman is all; and <i>akalmasam</i>,
who is taintless, free from vice etc.</p>
