O Kesava, <i>manye</i>, I accept; to be <i>rtam</i>, true indeed; <i>sarvam</i>, all; 
<i>etat</i>, this that has been said by sages and You; <i>yat</i>, which; <i>vadasi</i>, 
You tell, speak; <i>mam</i>, to Me. <i>Hi</i>, certainly; <i>bhagavan</i>, O Lord; 
<i>na devah</i>, neither the gods; <i>na danavah</i>, nor the demons; <i>viduh</i>, 
comprehend; <i>te</i>, Your; <i>vyaktim</i>, glory.
