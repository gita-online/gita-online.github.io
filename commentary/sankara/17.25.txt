After uttering the word <i>tat</i>, which is a name of Brahman, <i>yajna-tapah-kriyah</i>, acts of sacrifice and austerity; <i>ca</i>, as also; <i>vividhah</i>, the 
various; <i>dana-kriyah</i>, acts of charity, such as gift of land, gold, etc.; <i>kriyante</i>, are performed; <i>anabhisandhaya</i>, without regard for; 
<i>phalam</i>, results of actions; <i>moksa-kanksibhih</i>, by persons aspiring for Liberation. The use of the words Om and tat has been stated. Thereafter, 
the use of the word sat is bieng presently stated in the next verse.
