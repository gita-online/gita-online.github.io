O Partha, <i>sa</i>, that; <i>buddhih</i>, intellect; is <i>rajasi</i>, born of rajas; <i>yaya</i>, with which; 
<i>prajanati</i>, one understands; <i>ayathavat</i>, wrongly, not truly, not by discerning it from all points of 
view; <i>dharmam</i>, virtue, as prescribed by the scritpures; and <i>adharmam</i>, vice, what is prohibited by 
them; <i>ca eva</i>, as also; <i>karyam</i>, what ought to be done; and <i>akaryam</i>, what ought not to be 
done-those very 'duty' and 'what is not duty' as stated earlier.
