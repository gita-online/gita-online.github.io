<i>Ayudhanam</i>, among weapons; I am the <i>vajram</i>, thunderbolt, made of 
the bones of (the sage) Dadhici. <i>Dhenunam</i>, among milch cows; I am 
<i>kama-dhuk</i>, Kamadhenu, which was the yielder of all desires of (the sage) 
Vasistha; or it means a cow in general which gives milk at all times. 
I am <i>Kandarpa prajanah</i>, the Progenitor, (the god) Kama (Cupid). 
<i>Sarpanam</i>, among serpents, among the various serpents, I am <i>Vasuki</i>, 
the King of serpents.
