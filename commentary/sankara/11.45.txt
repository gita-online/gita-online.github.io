<i>Asmi</i>, I am; <i>hrsitah</i>, delighted; <i>drstva</i>, by seeing; <i>adrsta-purvam</i>, 
something not seen heretofore - by seeing this Cosmic form of Yours which has never been seen 
before by me or others. And <i>me</i>, my; manah, mind; is <i>pravyathitam</i>, stricken; 
<i>bhayena</i>, with fear. Therefore, deva, O Lord; <i>darsaya</i>, show; <i>me</i>, to me; 
<i>tat eva</i>, that very; <i>rupam</i>, form, which is of my friend. <i>Devesa</i>, O supreme God; 
<i>jagan-nivasa</i>, Abode of the Universe; <i>prasida</i>, be gracious!
