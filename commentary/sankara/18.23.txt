<i>Niyatam</i>, the daily obligatory; <i>karma</i>, action; <i>yat</i>, which; is <i>krtam</i>, performed; 
<i>sanga-rahitam</i>, without attachment; <i>araga-dvesatah</i>, without likes or dislikes; 
<i>aphala-prepsuna</i>, by one who does not hanker for rewards, by an agent who is the opposite of one who is 
desirous of the fruits of action; <i>tat</i>, that (action); ucyate, is said to be; <i>sattvikam</i>, born of sattva.
