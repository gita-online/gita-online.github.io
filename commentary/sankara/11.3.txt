<i>Parama-isvara</i>, O supreme Lord; <i>evam</i>, so; <i>etat</i>, it is - not otherwise; <i>yatha</i>, as; 
<i>tvam</i>, You; <i>attha</i>, speak; <i>atmanam</i>, about Yourself. Still, <i>purusottama</i>, O supreme 
Person; <i>iccahmi</i>, I wish; <i>drastum</i>, to see; the <i>aisvaram</i>, divine; <i>rupam</i>, form; <i>te</i>, 
of Yours, of Visnu, endowed with Knowledge, Sovereignty, Power, Strength, Valour and Formidability.
