<p><i>Sada yunjan</i>, by constantly concentrating;
<i>atmanam</i>, his mind; <i>evam</i>, thus, in the process
stated; <i>vigata-kalmasah</i>, the taintless, sinless yogi,
free from the obstacles to Yoga; <i>sukhena</i>, easily;
<i>asnute</i>, attains; <i>atayantam</i>, absolute - that which
exists by transcending limits -, supreme,
unsurpassable; <i>sukham</i>, Bliss; of <i>brahmasamsparsam</i>,
contact with Brahman - the Bliss that is
in touch with the supreme Brahman. Now is being shown
that result of Yoga which is the realization of
identity with Brahman and which is the cause of
the extinction of the whole mundane existence.
Liberation is conceived of in two ways - total
cessation of sorrows, and attainment of
unsurpassable Bliss.</p>
