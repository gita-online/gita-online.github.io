<i>Chalayatam</i>, of the fraudulent, of the deceitful; I am the <i>dyutam</i>, gambling, such as playing with 
dice. I am the <i>tejah</i>, splendour; <i>tejasvinam</i>, of the splendid. I am the <i>jayah</i>, victory 
of the victorious. I am the <i>vyavasayah</i>, effort of the persevering. I am the <i>sattvam</i>, virtue; 
<i>sattvavatam</i>, of the virtuous.
