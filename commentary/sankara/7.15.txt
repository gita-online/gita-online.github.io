<p><i>Mudhah</i>, the foolish; <i>duskrtinah</i>, evildoers,
sinners; who are <i>nara-adhamah</i>, the most
depraved among men; who are also <i>apa-hrtajnanah</i>,
deprived of, despoiled of (their) wisdom;
<i>mayaya</i>, by Maya; and <i>asritah</i>, who resort to;
<i>asuram bhavam</i>, demoniacal, ways, such as
cruelty, untruthfulness, etc.; <i>na</i>, do not;
<i>prapadyante</i>, take refuge; <i>man</i>, in Me, the supreme
God.</p>
