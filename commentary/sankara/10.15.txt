Since you are the origin of the Gods, and others, <i>Purusottama</i>, O supreme Person; 
<i>bhuta bhavana</i>, O Creator of beings, one who brings the creatures into being; 
<i>bhutesa</i>, the Lord of beings; <i>deva-deva</i>, O God of gods; <i>jagat-pate</i>, 
the Lord of the worlds; <i>tvam</i>, You; <i>svayam</i>, Yourself; <i>eva</i>, alone; 
<i>vettha</i>, know; <i>atmanam</i>, Yourself, as God possessed of unsurpassable powers 
of knowledge, sovereignty, strength, etc.; <i>atmana</i>, by Yourself.
