Not only will there be the giving up of your duty and fame, but <i>bhutani</i>, people; <i>ca api</i>, also; <i>kathayisyanti</i>, will speak; 
<i>te</i>, of your; <i>avyayam</i>, unending, perpetual; <i>akrtim</i>, infamy. <i>Ca</i>, and; <i>sambhavitasya</i>, to an honoured person, 
to a person honoured with such epithets as 'virtuous', 'heroic', etc.; <i>akirtih</i>, infamy; <i>atiricyate</i>, is worse than; <i>maranat</i>, death. The meaning is that, to anhonoured person death is perferable to infamy.
