Even men of wisdom who lack spiritual experience and  resort  to  using  the
mind as their only medium of  understanding  are  soon  overpowered  by  the
senses. What then is the impediment to those who take pride  in  the  bodily
conception? Lord Krishna  use  of  the  word  pramathini  meaning  turbulent
indicates one who is assailed by agitation.
