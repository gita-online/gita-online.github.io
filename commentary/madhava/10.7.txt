Lord Krishna  uses  the  word  <i>matva</i>  meaning  realisation  of  His  Supreme
Absolute position inspires one to perform <i>bhajante  mam</i>  or  perform  loving
devotion unto Him. This clears up any and all misconceptions  regarding  the
presence of individuality with the Supreme Lord.
