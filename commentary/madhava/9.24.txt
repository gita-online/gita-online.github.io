<p>
Although the Supreme Lord Krishna is the  object  of  all  propitiation  and
worship there is no relation or reciprocation by Him if  it  is  not  within
the <i>parampara</i> or authorised disciplic succession performed according to  the
prescribed injunctions of the Vedic scriptures.
