<p>
Lord Krishna uses the compound  word  <i>nirvana-paramam</i>  meaning  the  supreme
bliss which commences for an embodied being after  the  cessation  of  birth
and death in the physical body in the material existence and the  attainment
of the eternal spiritual nature.
