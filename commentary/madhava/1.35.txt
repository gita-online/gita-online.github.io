righteousness governing the  relationship  between  <i>brahmana</i>  the  spiritual
order<i>,  ksatriya</i>  the  warrior  order<i>,  vaisya</i>  the  mercantile  order   <i>and</i>
<i>sudra</i> the worker class are included here as well.
