Now  Lord  Krishna  reveals  the  essence,  the  most  confidential  of  all
knowledge  within Srimad Bhagavad Gita which will secure  permanent  release
from <i>samsara</i> the perpetual cycle of birth  and  death,  guarantee  <i>moksa</i>  or
liberation from material existence  and  propel  spiritual  ascendance  unto
eternal communion with the Supreme Lord.
