Here the words <i>vedais ca sarvam</i>  refers to  the  conclusions  of  the  Vedic
scriptures.  The  words  <i>vedanta-krt</i>  refers  to  Lord  Krishnas  <i>avatar</i>  or
incarnation of Vedavyasa the  author  of  the  Vedas,  Puranas  and  Vedanta
Sutras which all elucidate the absolute, ultimate truth.
