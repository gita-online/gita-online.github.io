The path of deliverance from <i>prakriti</i> or the material  substratum  pervading
physical existence and <i>moksa</i> or liberation from the material  existence  for
the <i>jivas</i> is achieved by embracing the 20 virtues given in verses  8  to  12
of  this  chapter  beginning  with  the  word  <i>amanitvam</i>  meaning   humilty,
reverence.
