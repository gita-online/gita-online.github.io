That is not all, there are innumerable warriors in great numbers in my  army
of  their  calibre.  <i>Bahavah</i>  meaning  innumerable  refers  to   Jayadratha,
Krtavarma, Salya and countless others who have all pledged their very  lives
for his sake and  due  to  their  sincere  affection  for  him,  with  their
extraordinary strength and their unsurpassed skill in military  science  his
victory is assured.
