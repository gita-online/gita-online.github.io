<i><u>Madhvacarya has no commentary so we present Baladeva Vidyabhusana's.</u></i>
<p>
The names Duryodhana has mentioned are: <i>Bhavah</i> referring to  Drona  himself.
Vikarna who is the younger brother of Duryodhana and Bhurisrava is  the  son
of Somadatta. <i>Samitinjayah</i> means ever victorious and is applied to  Bhishma,
Karna, Kripa and Asvatthama, the seven <i>maha-rathas</i> mentioned.
