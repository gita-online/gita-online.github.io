There  exists  numerous  reasons  why  a  person  is  impelled   to   sinful
activities. Here the word <i>atha</i> denotes which others.  Some  are  desire  and
anger. Arjuna wants to know which is the strongest impetus in provoking  one
to do unrighteous acts and which should by all means be avoided in order  of
priority.
<p>
Now begins the summation.
<p>
After the Supreme Lord the  demi-gods  are  the  most  powerful  mediums  of
influence. Now Arjuna wants to  know  what  other  influences  are  next  in
importance.
