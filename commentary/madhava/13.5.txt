Desire, repugnance, joy, misery, etc. are known as the modifications of  the
<i>ksetra.</i> The word <i>cetana</i> meaning awareness refers to  the  entire  perceptual
faculties of the mind. The word <i>sanghata</i> preceding  it  includes  perception
of the physical body and <i>cetana refers</i> to  the  total  aggregate  of  mental
facilities which includes modifications such as ego and patience.
