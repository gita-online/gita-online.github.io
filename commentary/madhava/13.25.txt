The word <i>eka-stham</i> meaning situated in the single material nature.  This  is
singular and denotes the Supreme Lord Krishna because He  is  all  pervading
throughout all  creation.   One  who  sees  all  <i>jivas</i>  or  embodied  beings
regardless of form or species as being part of  <i>prakkriti</i>  or  the  material
substratum pervading physical existence  which  is  a  modification  of  the
Supreme Lord and can  realise  the  difference  and  gradation  between  all
three; such a person  experiences  direct  realisation  of  the  <i>brahman</i>  or
spiritual substratum pervading all existence.
