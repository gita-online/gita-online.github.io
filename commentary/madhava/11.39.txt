Lord Krishna is addressed as <i>acyuta</i> meaning He who is infallible.  The  word
<i>ekah</i> refers to Him being the one and only, the best of all beings.  It  also
denotes the knower of all things <i>eka eva  karoti  yat</i>   which  means  as  He
alone does.
