<p>
Lord Krishna uses the compound word <i>yuktahara-viharasya</i>  means  one  who  is
temperate and regulated in their eating habits so that the efforts  to  feed
oneself  are  minimised  allowing  the  effort  to  achieve  perfection   in
meditation to be maximised.
