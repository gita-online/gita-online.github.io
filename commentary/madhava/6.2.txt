<p>
Renunciation is also included within the developed stages of <i>karma  yoga</i>  or
prescribed Vedic activities for  as  Lord  Krishna  states  if  desires  and
cravings are not abandoned then how is equanumity of actions possible?  This
is the underlying meaning of this verse.
<p>
Now begins the summation.
<p>
An advanced special attribute of <i>karma  yoga</i>  is  verily  the  attribute  of
renunciation of the rewards of action. This is the purport.
