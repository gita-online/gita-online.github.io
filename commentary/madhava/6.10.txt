Here Lord Krishna speaks on the process of  equanimity.  The  word  <i>yunjitah</i>
means concentrated in meditation and denotes that one should focus the  mind
on the soul by use of the word <i>atmanam</i> which can refer to the mind  as  well
as the soul.
