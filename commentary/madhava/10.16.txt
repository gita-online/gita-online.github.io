Although He is unborn yet the Supreme Lord Krishna removes the miseries  for
those who are born.Therefore  He  is  known  as  <i>Janardana</i>  the  remover  of
afflictions. The Bavahraya text describes <i>Janardana</i> as  the  supreme  unborn
remover of affliction.
