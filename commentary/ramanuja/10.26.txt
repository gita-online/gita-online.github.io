<p>
Lord Krishna's <i>vibhuti</i> or divine, transcendental opulence known as  <i>kamadhuk</i>
refers to <i>kamadhenu</i> or the divine and sacred Surabhi cows the  fulfiller  of
all desires. <i>Kandarpa</i>  is  His  <i>vibhuti</i>  which  is  the  primal  force  that
motivates procreation and causes  progeny.  <i>Sarpaanam</i>  refers  to  poisonous
single headed serpents of which <i>Vasuki</i> is king of them all.
