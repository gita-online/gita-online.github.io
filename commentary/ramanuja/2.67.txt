Therefore in the way  that  it  has  been  definitively  expounded  by  Lord
Krishna, whosoever achieves communion with Him the most exalted reality  for
meditation will surely  in  every  way  have  their  senses  abstracted  and
completely oblivious to the objects of the senses.   Thus  so  qualified  to
them will come installation of wisdom and so established  they  will  become
fit to attain the eternal soul. The achievement of one  who  has  subjugated
their senses and whose mind is situated in sublime serenity is discussed  in
the next verse.
