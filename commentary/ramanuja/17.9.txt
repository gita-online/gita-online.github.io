That <i>yagna</i> or acts of propitiation and worship which may  follow  the  Vedic
scriptures but is ostentatiously enacted out of desire for prestige  with  a
secret, greedy longing for enjoying the effects of rewards is  known  to  be
situated in <i>raja guna</i> the mode of passion.
