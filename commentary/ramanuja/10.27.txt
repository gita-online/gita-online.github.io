Lord Krishna reveals that His <i>vibhuti</i>  or  divine,  transcendental  opulence
among <i>naganam</i> is Ananta. <i>Naganam</i>  means  non-poisonous  serpents  with  many
heads. The word <i>yadasam</i> means  aquatics  or  inhabitants  of  water  and  of
punishers and chastisers His <i>vibhuti is</i> Yamaraja.
