Continuing Lord Krishna affirms that actions done  to  acquire  rewards  and
which are escorted with  conceit  and  attended  by  <i>ahankarena</i>  or  egoism,
thinking that one is the actual doer and desirous of everyone  to  know  and
which is accomplished only after great endeavour and expenditure  of  effort
is of the nature of <i>raja guna</i> the mode of passion.
