Others offer all the functions of the senses and all the  functions  of  the
<i>prana</i> or life breath along with the vital energy of the body in the fire  of
the purified soul ignited by knowledge. By  this  Lord  Krishna  means  that
such persons direct their efforts in disciplining the mind to  refrain  from
its pursuit of sensual activities.
