<p>
Lord Krishna uses the words <i>sarva-dvarani  samyamya</i>  means  controlling  the
passages which enter into the body.  This  denotes  withdrawing  the  senses
from  external  cognition  or  restricting  the  senses  from  their  normal
outgoing tendencies. The words <i>hrdi-nirudhya</i> means locking the mind  in  the
heart. It denotes directing devotional feelings from the mind  to  the  <i>atma</i>
or soul in the heart and  offering  them  to  the  <i>aksaram</i>  or  imperishable
Supreme Lord enthroned there. The  words  <i>yoga-dharanam</i>  or  performing  the
science  of  the  individual  consciousness  attaining  communion  with  the
ultimate consciousness in  unwavering  meditation.  This  denotes  concerted
concentration in perfectly meditating upon the Supreme  Lord  with  complete
attention.
