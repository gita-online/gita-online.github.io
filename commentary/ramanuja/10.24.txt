Of trees Lord Krishna's <i>vibhuti</i> or divine, transcendental  opulence  is  the
sacred Asvattha worshippable by everyone. Of elevated sages His  <i>vibhuti</i>  is
Narada because of  his  phenomenal  level  of  <i>bhakti</i>  or  exclusive  loving
devotion unto the Supreme Lord.
