Understanding Lord Krishna as delineated in  the  previous  verses  did  the
ancient seers and sages perform Vedic activities dedicated to Him  and  thus
achieved <i>moksa</i> or liberation from  material  existence.  He  is  encouraging
Arjuna to perform <i>karma yoga</i> or prescribed Vedic activities as the  ancients
such as Visvavan and Manu performed it in knowledge of Him.
