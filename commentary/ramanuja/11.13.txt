<p><div align=justify><p>
Perceiving the entire cosmos with unlimited wonders and marvels within  just
a part of Lord Krishna's <i>visvarupa</i> or divine universal form which  contained
innumerable glorious  qualities  and  attributes  such  as  omniscience  and
omnipotence etc. Arjuna with the hair on his head  standing  on  end  became
petrified with astonishment in rapturous exultation and  like  an  inanimate
rod fell prolong upon the earth prostrating  himself  with  head  bowed  and
hands folded in reverence exclaiming.
